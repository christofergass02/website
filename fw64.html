Fair World 64 is a text-based game of the 1964-1965 New York World's Fair.

To play, simply click 'Run' and follow the directions on the screen. The player will be asked to type their name, and 'left', 'right', 'yes', and 'no' to navigate through the fairgrounds.

There are three ways to complete the game; run out of money, run out of time, or reach the Lake Amusement Area or Transportation Area. Enjoy!




Interested to know more about Fair World 64?

More information regarding the research and creation of the game can be found in the project white paper at https://academicworks.cuny.edu/gc_etds/3786/
